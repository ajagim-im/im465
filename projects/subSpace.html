A sandwich making game where you put your abilities of making a sandwich to the test. Your goal is to catch as many falling ingredients as you can in the time that is given to you. The more that you catch means the higher your score will be. This game has achievements for the players that enjoy achievements. The game also has high scores where the player can try and achieve the highest score possible.
