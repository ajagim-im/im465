Office Fighter

Office Fighter is a mobile flash game created for apple and android phones. This game was created for my basic Interactive Media Authoring class with 3 other students in the fall of 2014. My work in the game includes assisting with the level design, programming, and user interface.

In this game the player is a normal office worker who must fight through ninjas to get to the elevator and try to escape the building. The player's weapons are all made of common day office items. These weapons are a broom, staple gun, and pencil crossbow.
